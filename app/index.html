Heatscape - Urban Heat Island Effect Solution

Discover: Primary & Secondary Research

The process began with secondary research to identify potential problem areas, followed by primary research including qualitative observations, ethnographic studies, and interviews in Sydney. Quantitative surveys were conducted to gauge public knowledge and habits during high-heat seasons.

Define: Thematic Analysis

The primary research was synthesized into key themes, revealing issues like the relation of heat to wealth, lack of public awareness, and the scarcity of free, cool spaces.

Develop: Ideation & Prototyping

Storyboards were used in the ideation process, and affinity diagramming and thematic analysis helped identify root issues for the prototype. Six iterations were conducted, each refining the core features and interface based on feedback.

Deliver: Final Prototyping & Testing

High-fidelity prototypes were developed and tested, leading to significant enhancements based on user feedback. The final design was developed into a web application, incorporating different APIs for an interactive user experience.

Iteration Details: Sketches to Wireframes to High Fidelity

Specific Methodologies and Processes

Final Design Elements

The final design features interactive elements like real-time body temperature visualization, simplified navigation, and personalized user experience.

CityGuard - Community Safety (Interaction Design Project)

Role: Interaction Designer | Tools: Figma, Otter AI, Jotform, Miro, Adobe After Effects, Dall-E 3, Loop11 for Remote Usability Testing

CityGuard is an innovative solution enhancing urban safety through crowdsourcing, focusing on crime prediction and analysis. This project demonstrates my skills in user-centric design and iterative development.

Comprehensive Research & Ethnography

Conducted in-depth user interviews and ethnographic studies, forming the basis of our user-centric approach and understanding of urban safety concerns.

Iterative Design & Prototyping (3 Rounds)

Employed an iterative design process, from initial sketches to wireframes, and high-fidelity prototypes, incorporating user and expert feedback in each round.

User Personas, Journey Mapping & Storyboarding

Developed user personas and journey maps post-ideation, followed by storyboarding to visualize potential user interactions.

Decision Matrix & Concept Selection

Utilized a decision matrix to evaluate and select CityGuard, focusing on multi-modal reporting and ethical data handling.

Basic Design System & Figma Prototyping

Developed a basic design system in Figma, crucial for maintaining consistency in design elements across the app.

User Testing & Feedback Integration

Conducted extensive user testing using cognitive walkthroughs and think-aloud protocols, systematically integrating feedback to enhance usability and user experience.

Ethical Considerations in Design

Addressed ethical design concerns, focusing on user privacy and data security. Developed an ethics canvas to guide our design decisions.

Final Design Overview & Impact

The final design of CityGuard features an intuitive user interface with multimodal interactions, reflecting my ability to create user-friendly and impactful solutions.
