About Me

I am a Design Computing graduate with a passion for creating stylish, intuitive, and user-friendly digital interfaces and experiences. My expertise includes rapid UX Design and deployment of applications in challenging environments like Indonesian slums, showcasing my versatility and problem-solving capabilities.

Additionally, I have successfully led my startup venture, 'Foodpile', to win a national pitch competition and secure funding, reflecting my entrepreneurial skills. Fluent in Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I am eager to apply my design skills in the fields of data, cloud services, blockchain, AI, and Web3 infrastructure, aiming to contribute to innovative and impactful tech solutions.
