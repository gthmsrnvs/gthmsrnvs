HeatScape

About the Project

Initiated in winter 2022, HeatScape addresses urban heat islands to enhance city comfort and health. This journey entailed leading design and development from concept to execution.

My Role

As Product Manager and Front-End Developer, my role encompassed UX/UI design, interaction design, and front-end development, integrating technologies like React.js, ROS 2, and Unity.

Design Process

Our process, rooted in the Double Diamond approach, involved extensive research, ideation, prototyping, and testing to create user-focused solutions.

Challenges and Solutions

Addressing challenges like a broad technology scope and team dynamics was crucial. Our methods ensured consistent progress and innovative solutions.

Key Accomplishments

Contributions led to significant UX advancements, including an adaptive web app and an engaging landing page redesign focused on user engagement.

Success Metrics

Our success was gauged through user engagement, inclusivity, and health outcomes, reflecting HeatScape's impact on urban living.
