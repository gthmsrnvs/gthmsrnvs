About Me

I am a Bachelor of Design Computing graduate with a passion for creating stylish, intuitive, and user-friendly digital interfaces and experiences. My expertise includes rapid UX Design and deployment of applications in challenging environments like informal settlements in Indonesia or national level pitch competitions at start-up incubators, showcasing my ability to work in fast-paced and high pressure environments.

Additionally, I have successfully led my startup venture, 'Foodpile', to win a national pitch competition and secure funding, reflecting my entrepreneurial spirit. Fluent in Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I am eager to apply my design skills in the fields of data, cloud services, blockchain, AI, and Web3 infrastructure, aiming to contribute towards innovative and impactful design in technology.
