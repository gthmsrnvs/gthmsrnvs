About Me

Hello! I'm Gautham, a UX Designer & Front-End Developer, and an alum of University of Sydney and UNSW. I specialize in creating intuitive, user-centric designs, leveraging my deep understanding of the double diamond design process and design thinking strategies.


My toolkit includes advanced proficiency in Figma, Adobe XD, Creative Cloud Suite and CAD for 3D modeling. I'm also adept with front-end technologies like React, and familiar with programming in SQL, Java, JavaScript, C++, HTML, CSS, and Python. I can use AI tools like Perplexity, Dall-E 3 and the latest OpenAI APIs in my design. This diverse skill set allows me to approach design challenges from multiple angles.


In my work, I apply user personas and UX research methodologies to gain insightful user behavior patterns. My expertise in heuristic evaluations and usability testing ensures each design is data-driven and user-focused, enhancing the overall user experience.


I aim to create digital solutions that are not only aesthetically engaging but also functionally superior, aligning with the latest trends in UI/UX design. If you're looking for a designer who can blend innovative design with cutting-edge technology, let's connect and collaborate!
