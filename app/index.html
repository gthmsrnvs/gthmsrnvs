Heatscape - Urban Heat Island Effect Solution

Role: UX/UI Designer | Tools: Figma, Adobe Suite, Jotform, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, User Testing Protocols

Background and Challenge

Heatscape addresses the Urban Heat Island effect with a focus on improving urban habitability. The project aligns with UN SDG 11.b and involved a deep dive into thermal comfort and urban climate.

Market Analysis and Synthesizing Insights

Conducted market analysis to identify gaps and opportunities for innovation, using thematic analysis to synthesize insights from a wide range of data sources.

Persona Development

Developed comprehensive personas that guided the design process, ensuring a user-centered approach to the solution.

Methodology and Iterative Design

Applied a structured double diamond design process, from initial concept sketches to interactive high-fidelity prototypes in Figma, tested and refined through multiple iterations based on user feedback.

Interactive Prototypes and User Testing

Developed interactive prototypes and conducted comprehensive user testing, employing cognitive walkthroughs, think-aloud protocols, and expert interviews to refine the design.

Final Design and Impact

The final design is a testament to empathetic UX/UI design with the potential to significantly impact urban living conditions.

Reflection and Future Work

This is an increasingly important problem with a solution can be evolved over time. This project demonstrates a blend of analytical skills, user empathy, and technical proficiency with a clear vision for future enhancements.

CityGuard - Community Safety (Interaction Design Project)

Role: Interaction Designer | Tools: Figma, Otter AI, Jotform, Miro, Adobe After Effects, Dall-E 3, Loop11 for Remote Usability Testing

CityGuard focuses on enhancing urban safety through crowdsourcing, targeting effective crime prediction and analysis.

Adapted Double Diamond Design Process

Utilized an adapted double diamond design process, encompassing Discover, Define, Develop, and Deliver phases for a comprehensive approach to the project.

Concept Selection with Decision Matrix

The concept was selected using a decision matrix, evaluating various options against set criteria to determine the most viable solution for development.

High-Fidelity Prototyping Iterations

1st Iteration: Focused on expert testing and heuristic evaluations, supported by affinity diagramming for synthesis of findings. 2nd Iteration: Included System Usability Scale (SUS) assessment and 'Think Aloud' protocol, with further refinement through an affinity diagram. 3rd Iteration: Involved comprehensive evaluation and expert feedback, refining the design process and decision-making.

Prototyping and Design System in Figma

Development of a prototyping and design system in Figma, post-ideation and prior to the third iteration, was crucial for creating a consistent and scalable design.

Research and Methodology

Employed a triangulated approach: surveys with Jotform, semi-structured interviews transcribed using Otter AI, and online ethnography. Ideation techniques like Crazy 8s, KJ-Method, and Reverse Thinking facilitated concept development.

Ethical and Inclusive Design

Focused on ethical considerations, including data privacy and security, to create a secure, privacy-conscious reporting platform.

Final Design Overview

The final high-fidelity prototype of CityGuard features user-centric design, leveraging multimodal and omnichannel interactions for effective crime prediction and analysis.
